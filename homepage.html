SYNOPSIS

Denji is a struggling teenager dreaming of a simple life whilst attempting to pay back his deceased father’s mountainous amount of debt to the Yakuza. Denji & his pet devil, Pochita attempt to make meets end by becoming a devil hunter! After Denji is betrayed by one of the only people he trusts, Pochita sacrifices himself and Denji becomes the infamous Chainsaw Man!

MEET FUJIMOTO

Tatsuki Fujimoto is a Japanese manga writer & artist who is most well known for his series ‘Chainsaw Man’ and ‘Fire Punch’ as well as many one-shots s such as ‘Look Back’.
