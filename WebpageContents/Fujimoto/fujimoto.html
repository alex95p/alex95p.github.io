Fujimoto

History
Tatsuki Fujimoto was born on October 10 in Nikaho, Japan. Fujimoto has said his influences were Korean Films, he explains in the terms of the 2008 fil The Chaser. He explains its not like your typical film, its different and always keep you on your toes! From a young age he partook in oil painting classes in his home time and had always aspired to be a manga artist, he also studied Western Painting at college.
Fujimoto has never been an assistant artist, he has been posting one-shot mangas since 2013 with his first release named Love is Blind which he won an honourable mention at the Shueisha Crown Newcomer's Award. He first gained popular traction was his manga series Fire Punch which ran for eight volumes in 2016. His first major hit was Chainsaw Man which has been running since 2018!

Fujimoto has a very small online presence, one of the only known videos of him is trying to levitate? It does match the wacky and weird world of his work though!
