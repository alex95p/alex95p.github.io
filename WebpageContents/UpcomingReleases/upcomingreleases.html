Chainsaw Man Film - Bomb Girl Arc
On December 17th 2023, the Bomb Girl arc film for the Chainsaw Man series was annnounced at Jump Fiesta '24. The film will be canon with the manga series and will follow Denji and his romantic relationship with Reze, a local Girl working at a small restaurant. The two grow closer together over time but is Reze really all she seems...?

Reze had been teased during the finale of Chainsaw Man season one, a faceless woman is seen walking towards a restaurant asking Denji if he would prefer life as a city or a country mouse. Reze's anime appearence created alot of hype around the Chainsaw Man community as it teased a newer season that fans had anticipated, as the most important scene throughout the Bomb Girl Arc had already been animated.


Voice Actor
Reze has both her Japanese and English voice actor confirmed from the season one finale. In the Japanese verison, Reze will be voiced by Reina Ueda who has also voiced Mao from the Pokemon Journeys: The Series as well as Kanao Tsuyuri from the critically acclaimed Demon Slayer. For the English dub, Reze will be voiced by Alexis Tipton, she has also had roles as Lucina throughout the Fire Emblem series and Kaguya Shinomiya from Love is War which was awarded 'Best Romance' at the Crunchyroll Anime Awards in 2023

Reina Ueda - Reze Voice Actor (Japanese) Alexis Tipton - Reze Voice Actor (English)

Look Back Film Adaption

Look Back is one-shot manga written by Tatsuki Fujimoto (one-shot means the entire story is told in one volume). The story follows two young aspiring manga artists who draw for their school newspaper as the two help inspire each other. However nothing can protect them from the terrible tragedy ahead.
Currrently Look Back does not have a UK theater release date, but will be released in Japan on June 28th 2024.
