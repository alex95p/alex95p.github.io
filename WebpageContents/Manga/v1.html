Plot Synopsis
Denji is a teenager living in extreme poverty after the death of his both of his parents. Prior to his Father’s death, he was immense debt to Yakuza which has been left up to Denji to make ends meet. With the help of his pet devil, Pochita, Denji becomes a devil hunter in order to hunt wanted devils throughout Japan!
