Episode Synopsis

After Denji was too ill to make his own way home from the drinks, he is awoken in Himeno's appartment. The two share breakfast and bond over their crazy lifestyles as devil hunters!
Makima is on a train with her associate to Kyoto to meet with higher-ups, suddenly the pair are ambushed by a large group of men who shot them both and have started their operation.
In Tokyo, each members of division 4 are on their own seperate missions. Denji, Power, Aki and Himeno are seen in a local small ramen shop, a man at a nearby table strikes up conversation with the group about the quality of the food. He suddenly reveal that is Debt Collector's grandson and is seeking revenge on Denji after his grandfather's debt! He reveals a gun and begins shooting but Aki summons his Fox Devil in order to defeat the gentleman, but he emerges as Katana Man! Aki then uses curse devil and on the count of 0 and kills the man.
In an attempt to aid Aki Himeno summons the Ghost devil and demands she gives it her 'everything'. As the battles continue, Himeno slowly begins to fade away, the scary woman summons the snake devil biting off the Ghost Devil's head and Aki realises that Himeno has died.
