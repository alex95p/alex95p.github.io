Dog & Chainsaw

Episode Synopsis

Denji is a young teenager struggling in a life of poverty after the death of his parents. After the death of his father, the Yakuza has forced Denji to repay his Father's mountainous amounts of debt. In order to do this, Denji becomes a devil hunter with the help of his pet devil, Pochita! The two work as a duo as they dream of a simple life. After Denji is betrayed by the Yakuza who attempt to kill him in order to gain a contract with the Zombie Devil which will allow the Yakuza to gain the Zombie Devil's powers.

During battle, Denji is severly injured and is close to death... However Pochita sacrifes himself in order for Denji to live out the life they have always dreamed of together! Pochita becomes Denji's heart, letting him become a human devil hybrid known as Chainsaw Man! After Denji soars back into battle and is able to take out the large hoard of zombie devils, he is left exhausted and suffers from a loss of blood. The ending of the episode shows a mysterious trio entering the abandoned warehouse where the battle took place, Makima, a leader of Public Safety throughout Japan, takes Denji in in order to protect him.
