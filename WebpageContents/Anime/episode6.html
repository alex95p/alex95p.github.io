Episode Synopsis

The group begin to panic and start trying to find a way out of the 8th floor, all windows lead to other rooms and they realise they are DEFINTELY stuck! The group ponder and realise this is most likely a devils doing, they even try going through the ceiling and realise it leads them back to the 8th floor. They all begin to argue and blame each other for the devils doing and Kobeni cries in panic.Aki has realised all the clocks are stuck at the same time, realising they are not stuck in the 8th floor, more that they are stuck within an endless time loop!
The group then hear a noise and go to investigate, a small blob like devil appears and demands that group forms a contract with it and then demands for Denji, Kobeni is in a state of panic and grabs a knife and states they should kill Denji in order to escape! The group go back and forth deciding their next move, the devil then grows larger and begins to chase them and announces it is in fact the Eternity Devil! Denji makes the rash decision and decides to venture into the hallway and activates his chainsaw powers!
