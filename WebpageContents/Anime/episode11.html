Episode Synopsis

As Aki enters the cell, the Future Devil keeps chanting 'The future rules!!', Aki explains he would like to form a contract with him. He states he wants to see Aki's future first before deciding on a contract and demands he sticks his head into his gut in order to see it. The Future Devil cackles and demands Aki's right eye in order to bind their contract, he tries to inform Aki of his death but he stops him before he can say and the devil enters his eye.
After Kishibe defeats our pair once again, he says they will be taking part in special combat training in order to battle with Katana Man and Akane. Katana Man and Akane are seen in their hideout and realise they cannot hide forever. Makima meets with the current Yakuza mob and learns they have made deals with the Gun Devil for money, Makima demands everyone involved anf their families names, she reveals a large bag. The men mock her and say they wont take any bribes from but she opens the bag and to is everyone's disgust it is everyone in the room's famillies eyes and demands to meet the man in charge.
Special Divison 4 are seen surrounding Katana Man's hideout, they enter and are met with members of Public Safety. They are all fiends, devil human hybrids named Beam, Angel and Princi. Aki investigates the upper levels after the group defeat a large hord of zombie devils. He is greeted with Akane and Snake Devil but uses his Future devil's Power to see into the near future but is being choked by the Snake Devil!!
