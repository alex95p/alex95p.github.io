Gun Devil

Episode Synopsis

The next day, Denji visits Makima's office, she explains she has a request that he defeats the Gun Devil. She explains the last sighting was 13 years ago but hasn't been seen since, she believes that Denji has the Power in order to defeat it!
A flashback shows a young Aki, playing outside his childhood home with his older brother having a snowball fight. His brother says his hands are getting colder and rushes inside to grab a pair of gloves, whilst inside the house the Gun Devil strikes and destroys his home alongside killing all his family inside. She goes onto to explain the castrophic impact that the Gun Devil's last sighting had and explains they need to find fragments to create a piece large enough to attract it.
Division 4 are assigned a new mission to investgate the Hotel Morin, which has leads linking to the Gun Devil. Aki's fragment confirms this on the groups arrival. The group head inside and decide to investigate and begin wandering the endless hallways throughout the hotel. They hear noise and see a small devil approach from one of the hotel rooms which leaps towards the group. Himeno, Aki's first partner, uses her Ghost Devil ability in order to protect Kobeni. The group begin to head upstairs and realise they are stuck on the 8th floor of the hotel!.
