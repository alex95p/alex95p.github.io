Episode Synopsis

In a flashback, we see the deal exchanged between Power and the Bat Devil meaning that Power has to bring her a human in order to get her beloved Meowy back! She is awaken after being saved by Denji and realises she is back with Meowy and is confused as why he would save her after she was so cruel. Suddenly Power realises shes unable to move and demands denji takes Meowy in order to save them.
With a small amount of blood left, Denji begins to struggle to fight the Leech Devil who has appeared after the altercation with the Bat Devil, but Denji eventually manages to attack her tongue but she grasps onto Denji. Out of nowehere Aki appears and uses his Fox Devil with the power of Kon in order to save the trio! Denji wakes up in hospital and Aki explains the situation to him following the intense events aftermath. The next morning, the two are quietly going about their day and hear a loud pounding at the door but before they can reach it Power has pounded down the door and announces shes been announced to stay with the pair!
