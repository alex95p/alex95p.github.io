The Taste of a Kiss

Episode Synopsis

Denji lunges at the devil and begins to slash but is causing no injury, the Eternity Devil manages to knock out Denji and believes it has won, but Himeno uses her Ghost Devil once again and pulls Denji's cord to reactivate his chainsaw powers, with Denji's crazy plan to attack it from the inside out he defeats it and the group successfully escape the hotel!
Denji has discovered a piece of the Gun Devil and the group decide to have celebratory drinks in order to celebrate their victory!

Episode Clip

This clip from the official CrunchyRoll youtube channel shows the heated showdown between Denji and the Eternity Devil after fighting for three days! He realises the perfect way to defeat it and lunges at it like the mad man he is!
