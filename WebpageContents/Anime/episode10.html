Bruised and Battered

Episode Synopsis

Aki is awoken in hospital after the intense fight with Katana Man, he realises Himeno is actually gone and begins to cry and mourn the loss of his partner. After Denji and Power leave the hospital after visiting Aki Makima asks them to meet up with her at the graveyard. At the graveyard the two are introduced to Kishibe, a Special Division 1 officier who has offered to train the pair in order to protect themselves better!
Kishibe states that he will be at their appartment the next morning in order to discuss further training. The conduct a plan which they have named 'Operation Super Smart', which is to prove that they are already strong enough. The plan goes terribly wrong and Kishibe suggests they should use their brains more when fighting.
Aki is taken to hidden secret facility to have a discussion with a stronger devil after losing his contract with the fox devil. Aki is greeted by the Future devil as is cautiously wanders into one of the cells.
