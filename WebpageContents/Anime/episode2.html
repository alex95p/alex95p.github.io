Arrival in Tokyo

Episode Synopsis

After Makima takes Denji in, they stop off at a rest area to eat after they discover Denji has been living in poverty. Not long later, the group arrives in Tokyo and Makima shows Denji round Public Safety HQ, an institution established after the rise of devil sightings and threats throughout Japan. Makima introduces Denji to Aki Hayakawa and the two are assigned to work together after the discovery of Denji's devil abilities. The two DO NOT get along, they end up pickering and havign a physical fight in an alleyway! The pair return back to HQ and Makima decides Denji should join Aki's unit which Aki is NOT PLEASED about due to Aki's devil hatred, especially after Aki is assigned to live with him in order to keep an eye on him. The two constantly clash due to Denji's messiness and Aki's cleaniness. That day they are assigned their first mission together to investigate a Fiend reporting. Denji is unsure about harming the fiend after Denji has learnt that devils can be your friends, with hestitation Denji kills the fiend.

Upon returning to Public Safety HQ, Makima explains Denji will be assigned a new partner named Power. She explains that theyre often small patrol teams. The pair begin the patrol and Power's big personaility and desire for blood due to being a fiend starts to frustrate Denji. She runs off after smelling a scent, she spots the Sea Cucumber Devil and jumps from a massive building in order to defeat it.
