Meowy's Whereabouts

Episode Synopsis

After Power ruthlessly attacking the Sea Cucumber Devil, Makima warns them about Power's fooliness and that they should be more hesitant and careful in case they harm an innocent civilian. Two begin making up lies in order to make them appear better and begin to bicker. Makima silences them by wanting them to work well together. The two are then seen by a vending machine buying drinks, Power spots a cat nearby and starts to stroke it. Power explains her pet cat, Meowy, was kidnapped by a devil, before being able to get Meowy back, she was taken in by Makima to join public safety. Denji sympathies with her due to his loss of Pochita and the pair venture out to get Meowy back!
On the journey there, Power explains she knows the devils hideout and reckons Denji could take the devil down! They reach the countryside outside of Tokyo and venture towards an abandoned house, Power suddenly strikes Denji with her blood hammer and claims 'humans are so foolish', she takes Denji inside and attempts to trade him to the Bat Devil in order to get Meowy back, as per the previous discussed deal. The bat devil tastes Denji's blood and immediately is disgusted due to it tasting like cigarettes.
The bat devil begins to fly towards Tokyo to get better human blood, but Denji is latched onto her back. The two land in Tokyo and begin to have an intense fight using cars and buildings to fight with!
