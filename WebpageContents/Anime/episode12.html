Episode Synopsis

As Aki reminises on a memory of Himeni, the Ghost Devil sets Aki free handing him a cigarette of the same brand Himeno used to smoke. Remembering the words of Himeno, he climbs to the devils head and beheads it. Akane attempts to intereven but Kobeni appears behind her with a knife
Power and Denji are seen in a lift bickering as usual, they arrive at the 7th floor in silence so the Zombie devils dont hear them but Power loudly challenges them all to FIGHT and Denji leaves Power to fight the devils on her own. As Deni arrives on a higher floor he is greeted by Katana man and two of his henchmen. The two begin to fight and fly off a building landing on a train, after a long brawl Denji is able to defeat Katana Man and ties him tied to the train and denji says the police are already on their way.
After the end of the Katana Man arc, Makima reveals Akane was an ex-devil hunter who had a contract with the Gun Devil which she was using to give weapons to the Yakuza.
The ending sequence follows a girl who questions Denji's lifestyle, whether he is a city mouse or a country one...?
