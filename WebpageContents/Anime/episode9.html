From Kyoto

Episode Synopsis

After stating that Katana Man wants Denji's heart, the two begin to brawl! One of Katana Man's henchmen call for backup and two gentlemen arrive to join the battle!
On Makima's train, it appears the group have died due to the ambush attack, HOWEVER Makima stands up! Kurose and Tendo patiently await for Makima's arrival but are worried as they have heard about the altercation, the two are bamboozled when Makima walks out the train looking untouched but covered in blood stating all the attackers have died. Makima is informed about the situation in Tokyo and realises they will be after Denji, she demands 30 convicts and to take them to Kyoto's highest point. They arrive at a shrine and Makima has cleaned up, the convicts are all blinded infront of the shrine. She approaches each of the convicts and asks them to repeat the name she tells them.
Meanwhile in Tokyo, Katana Man's posse are all gathered, they start to one by one combust, pertified the group begin to run. Akane suggests to Katana Man these killings are Makima's doing, in the distance they spot a scared Kobeni holding a knife. She dashes towards the pair and slices Katana Man's arm, the two scramble into their van and Kobeni states 'this job is making me crazy!'
Upon Makima's return to Tokyo she learns she is officially in charge of division 4!

Episode Clip

This clip from the official Crunchyroll Youtube channel shows Makima coming to Division 4's aid after hearing about the incident in Tokyo from Kyoto. She showcases her crazy powers like we haven't seen before in order to kill many of Katana Man's henchmen!
